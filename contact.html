Frequently asked questions

What info helps you quote faster?

Share the vehicle make/model, colour, known defects, preferred timeframe, and whether you’d like mobile or studio service. Photos of the paintwork or cabin always help.

Can you visit my location?

Yes. We’re mobile across greater Cape Town with a self-contained setup. Secure water/electricity access needed.

How do I secure a booking?

Once we send your tailored plan, simply approve on WhatsApp or email. A deposit may apply for multi-day paint correction or coating work.

What payment options are available?

EFT and on-site card facilities are preferred. Cash is accepted for mobile visits. Tax invoices are supplied on request.
