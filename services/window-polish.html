How to know it is time to polish your glass

Modern glass is tough, but road film and wiper wear slowly reduce night visibility. These are the triggers we look for before recommending machine polishing.

Sign 01

Wiper trail glare

Wiper blades streak across your windscreen at night even after a wash.

Sign 02

Mineral spotting

Hard water dots on glass that you cannot scrape off with clay or razor blades.

Sign 03

Sticky wipers

Blades chatter across the glass due to roughness or etched deposits.

Sign 04

Camera/ADAS glare

Driver-assist systems struggle at night because of internal reflections.

What Causes It

Cape Town elements bake onto glass fast

Sea spray, borehole water and mountain dust mingle on glass. Combined with wiper use, they leave microscopic scratches and mineral haze.

  • Hard water rinses leave calcium that bonds with heat.
  • Improper wiper replacement drags metal clips over the glass.
  • Off-road dust mixes with rain and etches in seconds.
