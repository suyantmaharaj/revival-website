Signs your interior needs more than a quick tidy

Cabins quietly collect DNA from every commute. Here is how to tell it is time for a Revival-level reset.

Sign 01

Leather looks chalky

UV exposure and harsh cleaners leave steering wheels grey and shiny.

Sign 02

Allergens linger

Dust trapped in seats and HVAC vents triggers sneezing.

Sign 03

Sticky touchpoints

Spills in cup holders, buttons and screens demand targeted agitation.

Sign 04

Glass haze at night

Interior film diffuses headlights and indicates constant off-gassing.

What Causes It

Cape Town lifestyle hits every surface differently

Beach trips, school runs and coffee commutes stack oils, sunscreen residue and fine dust on leather, alcantara and piano black trim.

  • Sea air & humidity leave salt crystals that dry out stitching.
  • Red dust + pollen embed deep into fabric weave and HVAC ducts.
  • Product build-up from supermarket wipes creates shiny, slippery plastics.
