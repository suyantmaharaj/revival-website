When your headlights are due for restoration

Polycarbonate lenses degrade slowly, so you get used to hazy light output. These signs mean it’s time for a Revival reset.

Sign 01

Yellow or cloudy lenses

Oxidation turns clear lenses amber, diffusing light and failing roadworthy checks.

Sign 02

Night glare + dim beams

Light scatters onto the lens instead of down-road, reducing visibility.

Sign 03

Rough texture

You can feel the oxidation layer with your fingernail even after washing.

Sign 04

Failed inspection

Roadworthy technicians flag the haze and require restoration or replacement.

What Causes It

Harsh UV, salt and poorly applied coatings

Cape Town sun and coastal air break down the factory UV layer. Quick fixes like wipe-on coatings often peel, leaving patchy lenses.

  • UV exposure oxidises the polycarbonate surface.
  • Sea spray + road salt accelerate yellowing and pitting.
  • DIY sanding leaves scratches without sealing properly.
